# Follow-up

1. Identify whether this is after applying, after an interview, or after a missed reply.
2. Draft a subject line and a message under 130 words.
3. Mention one specific point from the role or conversation if it was provided.
4. Keep the request easy to answer.
5. Recommend when to send it, but never send it or claim it was sent.

Do not guess the recipient's name or email address.
