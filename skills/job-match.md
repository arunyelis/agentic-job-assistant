# Job match

1. Extract the role title, company, seniority, location, and five most important requirements.
2. Match each requirement to evidence in the candidate profile.
3. Label missing evidence as a gap. Do not turn adjacent experience into direct experience.
4. Give a fit summary using strong match, partial match, or weak match. Explain why in plain language.
5. Suggest three honest resume changes and three interview questions to prepare for.

If the job description or candidate profile is incomplete, say what is missing.
